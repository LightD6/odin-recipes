Nachos

Description

A cheesy plate of nachos filled with your choice of meat and a spicy kick. Perfect for the whole family to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4lbs Ground Beef (or your choice of meat)
  • 3 Jars of Queso
  • 1 Large Jar of Picante Sauce
  • Bag of Tortilla Chips
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Tbsp of Tony's Creole Seasoning

Steps

  1. Chop whole onion
  2. Turn stove to medium low heat
  3. In a skillet add the chopped onions, meat and Tony's
  4. Cook until meat browns and fully cooks through, make sure onions are soft
  5. Remove meat and drain any fat in the pan
  6. Add meat back into the pan and add Picante and Queso
  7. Bring to a boil then lower heat and let simmer while stirring constantly
  8. Remove from heat and add to the tortilla chips
  9. Add any toppings that you deem fit and enjoy!
